SAFETY

We’ll catch you when you fall with our groundbreaking ‘roll over sensor’ that will trigger the powertrain isolation in the event of a vehicle rollover.

This is basically an in-built system that will prevent you from accelerating the scooter in a rolled over condition and will resume acceleration only when the scooter has been raised back to a comfortable position.

For extra added safety, the side stand sensor will only shift from parking mode to drive mode once the side stand is removed.

Home is not too far away with our ‘limp-home feature’. In case of any abnormal behaviour in your vehicle, D15 will shift to Limp Mode and move at a reduced speed to reach you to our service center, a safe spot or any place you’d like to call home for quick help!

This means you can always count on your BG D15 to sense a faulty error and get you home safely without stopping mid commute.

The control is always in your hands! Go into drive mode, whenever you please by pressing the Brake + Drive Switch after turning on the vehicle.A special feature for a safer start to a better future!
